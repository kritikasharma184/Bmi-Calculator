WHAT IS BMI?

BMI stands for Body Mass Index. It's a measure that uses a person's height and weight to determine if they are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. It's calculated by dividing a person's weight in kilograms by the square of their height in meters (BMI = weight (kg) / height (m)^2).
